Mascot of Pokémon Games: Pikachu

Pikachu is popularly known as the mascot of the Pokémon franchise and a major representative of Nintendo's collective mascots. It is also the game mascot and starter Pokémon of Pokémon Yellow and Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu!
It has made numerous appearances on the boxes of spin-off titles. Pikachu is the most famous Pokémon, making the Pokémon cartoons popular worldwide.
